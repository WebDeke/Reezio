Give us a smile.

Did you know? It's scientifically proven that if you force yourself to smile, even if you're not happy, you will begin to feel happier! You can read more about it here.

Directions:

1. Make sure you are not too close or too far from the camera. A good way to measure this is to extend your arm out to your webcam. It should be barely touching the lens.

2. Turn on some lights! This won't work well if you are in a dim room so make sure you have some good lighting while you try this out.

3. Stay centered. Do not tilt your head to the left or the right! Pretend you are taking a school yearbook picture and just smile straight at the camera.

4. Show us some teeth!

Once you press the button, give it a few seconds to load. After that, try to get your smile score to at least a 0.80 or 0.90!
